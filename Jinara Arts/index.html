About Jinara

A Passion Painted
With Every Stroke

Jinara is a Kerala-based fine art studio dedicated to the ancient, soul-stirring medium of oil paint. With a deep reverence for classical technique and a contemporary eye, each painting is handcrafted with the finest pigments on archival canvas.

Whether it is a sweeping landscape, a cherished portrait, or an abstract expression of feeling — every custom painting begins with listening to your story and ends with a painting that lives and breathes on your wall.
